Hair Care Guide

How to Care for Your Extensions:

  1. Washing: Use sulfate-free shampoo and lukewarm water. Wash gently from top to bottom.
  2. Conditioning: Apply deep conditioner weekly to maintain softness and shine.
  3. Drying: Air dry or use low heat settings. Avoid high heat directly on extensions.
  4. Styling: Use heat protectant spray before styling with hot tools.
  5. Storage: Keep extensions in a cool, dry place when not in use.
  6. Detangling: Always detangle from bottom up using a wide-tooth comb.
